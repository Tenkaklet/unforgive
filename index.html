About

Unforgiving – A Northern Hymn is a first person survival horror game for PC. Finding yourself in the deep Swedish woods, a box of matches is the only thing that can offer some comfort from the darkness.

The various settings of Unforgiving are based on the Swedish folklore with all its vicious creatures and stories, joined with the magic of Nordic shamanism.

Genre: Survival Horror
Platform: PC
Release date: Q4 2017

Team

Lead Designer: Gustav Edberg

Gustav studies design from a game development perspective. His earlier experience includes being the project leader on both Notes of Obsession (2016) and Rangor (2015), as well as being a designer on both of those games. On Unforgiving, Gustav is the Project leader, game writer and game designer.

Audio Director: William sahl

William studies Audio and Music from a game development perspective. He was the Lead sounddesigner for Notes of Obsession (2016). On Unforgiving, William is responsible for both sound and music, as the games audio director.

Lead Artist: John Kalderon

John studies graphics and 3D design from a game development perspective. Before that, John worked with film editing and market design. He was the 3D- and lead artist of Notes of Obsession (2016). On Unforgiving, John is the 3D- and lead artist.

Lead Animator: Caroline Petri

Caroline studies graphics and animation from a game development perspective. Her earlier experiences include being a lead artist and animator for Cria (2016) and Adals Vikings (2015), working in marketing design, sales and as a painting artist. On Unforgiving, Caroline is the lead animator and 2D artist.

Lead Programmer: Carl Pettersson

Carl studies programming with a game development perspective. His earlier work includes making the game Electric Racing (2015), as well as being a programmer on Notes of Obsession (2016). On Unforgiving, Carl is the lead programmer
